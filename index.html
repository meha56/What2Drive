About

(All information is as of December 31, 2023)

Toyota Motor Corporation , more commonly known as Toyota, is a Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer headquartered in Toyota City, Aichi, Japan. Toyota is the largest automobile manufacturer in the world (producing about 10 million vehicles per year) and was deemed the most valuable car brand in the world in the Interbrand Global Best Brands 2023 report. Toyota Motor Corporation owns Toyota, Daihatsu and Lexus and was founded Kiichiro Toyoda on 28th August, 1937. Toyota Motor Corporation's capital (as of March 31, 2023) is 635 billion yen and it's annual revenue of 2023 was $274.942B, a 1.55% decline from 2022. Toyota has expanded from merely automobile manufacturing to housing, financial services, communications, marine and biotechnology and afforestation.

Sustainability

From Mercedes-Benz Sustainability Report 2022 and Mercedes-Benz Official Websites

Mercedes-Benz views shaping the future of mobility in a safe and sustainable manner as motivation and duty. Therefore, the company focus's on innovative and green technologies alongside safe and high-quality vehicles. Mercedes countinues to invest and develop for an all-electric future. It's sustainability targets and initiatives are as follows:

  • Mercedes-Benz is shifting from electric-first to electric-only and is accelerating towards a fully-electric and software driven future.
  • Mercedes-AMG's Formula One Team's aim is to become the world's most sustainable team with technological innovation, pioneering spirit and moral obligation and recieved a 21% reduction in overall air travel emissions and 46% reduction in race team travel emissions in 2022.
  • Mercedes-Benz is building it's own battery recycling factory to conserve valuable resources which will recieve a recycling rate of over 96%.
  • Mercedes-Benz targets to cover more than 70% of the energy demand in production with renewable energies (especially through the expansion of solar and wind energy at it's sites) by 2030.

Toyota GR-Supra

details

The Toyota GR-Supra is an agile and powerful sportscar and multiple award winner (Drive Sports Car of the Year 2020 and West Wheels Sports Car of the Year 2019). It offers various unique color choices and both GT and GTS grades. Additionally, it has finely tuned aerodynamics and adaptive six lens headlights. In the interior, it provides a MID Instrument Display, advanced color touchscreen and advanced safety technology.

Review:
From the interior, the GR-Supra implements a high standard of usability in technology with its high resolution touch screen interface, embedded satellite navigation and buttons conveniently placed near the steering wheel. Additionally, everything is elegantly finished in a gloss carbon-firbre which seperates this car from the rest. Although this car can go extremely fast, it may not be suitable to be driven just in the city. Furthermore, it can cost between $85,000 - $110,000. Therefore, the GR Supra offers many features of a sports car at an affordable price but may not be suitable depending on use of the car.

Toyota RAV4-Prime

details

The RAV4-Prime is Toyota's electric SUV with a hybrid battery warranty. On the exterior, it has an unique front end and uses two-tone paint to elevate the style and aesthetics. On the interior each of the seats are wrapped in premium SofTex-trimmed material to emphasise comfort and there is a premium leather trim to give the SUV a sporty look. Additionally, there is a panoramic sliding glass roof and ambient lighting options.

Review:
Since the SUV is an electric car, it is sustainable and only requires 4.5 hours of charging time for a long all electric driving range. Additionally, it is extremely comfortable and is spacious for passengers and to keep luggage. However, the steering wheel lacks the convenience and comfort compared to the other cars.

Toyota Corolla Sedan

details

The Corolla Sedan's design is unique with a sport-like poise. On the exterior, every line and surface has been shaped to redefine this sedan and the flared wheels arche to enhance it's sporty look. On the interior, it implements ambient lighting, 1 year of complementary access to Toyota Connected Services, a head-up display, keyless entry and a touch screen multimedia. It also uses modern features such as active cruise control, lane trace assist, wpeed limit warnings, a reversing camera and passive SRS airbags.

Review:
Toyota's Corolla Sedan tries to create a new style of sedan's with its unique shape and design. It requires limited fuel consumption in prime conditions and is easy to drive even over rough bumps. Additionally, it is extremely spacious and has a large trunk. However, the desing isn't too visually appealing and there are no parking sensors or folding mirrors. Therefore, the Corolla Sedan is good for someone looking for a spacious, comfortable and affordable car which is easy to maintain.
