About

(All information is as of March 31, 2023)

Mazda Motor Corporation, also known as Mazda, is multinational automotive manufacturer based in Japan and headquartered in Fuchu, Hiroshima. Mazda's main buisness is the manufacturing and sales of passenger cars and comercial vehicles. Mazda was founded on January 30, 1920 by Jujiro Matsuda. Mazda's capital is 284 billion Yen. Mazda has 9 production sites in China (2), Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia. Mexico, U.S.A and Japan and 5 Research and Development Sites in China, U.S.A and Japan (3). Mazda has had sales in over 130 countries and regions and it's global production is 1,569,665 units. Mazda's global sales are 1,561,509 units and employs 49,998 consolidate employees and 23,087 unconsolidated empoyees. Mazda's main shareholders are Japan Trustee Services Bank, Ltd (Trust), The Master Trust Bank of Japan, Ltd (Trust), Toyota Motor Corporation, Japan Trustee Services Bank, Ltd (Trust 9), Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation, Japan Trustee Bank, Ltd (Trust 5), BNYMSANV AS AGENT/CLIENTS LUX UCITS NON TREATY 1, Japan Trustee Services Bank (Trust 7), HSBC BANK PLC A/C CLIENTS 1 and STATE STREET BANK WEST CLIENT-TREATY 505234.

Sustainability

From Mazda Sustainability Report 2023

Mazda is increasing it's sustainable growth as a global manufacturing company through their global buisiness activities. Mazda is aiming to prevent global warming through conservation intiatives and sound material-cyles. It's sustainability initiatives are as follows:

  • An action plan is currently (as of March 31, 2023) being developed to ensure and implement sustainable materials which are in-line with both, Mazda's stakeholders and a detail of surverys conducted by global ESG rating organisations.
  • Mazda has established a sustainability initiative promotion organisation through-out the entire value chain in cooperation with supplies and dealerships and complies with international rules, laws and regulations.
  • Each department in Mazda carries out it's operations based on goals and plans created by policies and guidelines determined by CRS Management Strategy Committe, which the president chairs and in cooperation with other Group companies.

Mazda MX-5

details

Offers a Skyactiv-G petrol engine, 8.8-inch full color touch screen display, rear-wheel drive, premium unleaded petrol and a 2 seat configuration.

Review:
The minimalistic sports car look and macho Gunmental Grey 17-inch BBS forged alloy wheels make the Mazda MX-5 an aesthetic model. However, the MX-5 is a very small sportscar and can be tight, especially for anyone over six-feet, but may be preferable if you are looking to drive solo. The fuel efficiency is great for a sports car and it includes four airbags(dual and front side) alongside other safety systems. Overall, Mazda MX-5's are one of the finest sports cars on the market and is the perfect in terms of affordability and packaging for someone who prioritises drives.

Mazda CX-5

details

Offers comfortable room for five people, ergonomic seats that support the curvature in your back and is crafted to capture light and reflect its surrounding environment.

Review:
The CX-5 comes with a plethora of specifications such as front wheel drive, all wheel drive, three petrol engines and a diesel. The CX-5 includes six airbags for safety alongside a speed limiter and other safety technology and the seats are heated. Although it includes two cup holders, a charging mat and a decent cabin size, the colors of the interiors of the CX-5 are not as lifting or aesthetic as other models or brands. On the road the CX-5 is a super relaxed car and delivers a swift and smooth drive. In conclusion, the Mazda CX-5 gives plenty of luxury and comfort for someone looking for a calm interior, smooth looks and a subtly powerful engine.

Mazda6 Sedan

details

Offers an 8-inch full color touch screen display, 474 liters of cargo room, 2.5 liter petrol engine and a 2.5 liter petrol turbo engine.

Review:
The Mazda 6 offers a medium sized passenger sedan rivaling the Volkswagen Passat and Toyota Camry. The aesthetics of the sedan may not be as high tech or stylish as its other designs, however, its simplistic design thrives in darker colors such as black or blue. The simplistic style also transfers to the button design and makes it easy and convenient to meddle with. Additionally, the Mazda 6 provides a comfortable amount of leg room and space for tall passengers or drivers, specifically in the front. Furthermore, it grants 2 USB-A ports, an auxillary input, two cup-holders and a wireless phone charger. Although, its display is only touch-enables when the car is still, the sedan still implements the 11-speaker Bose systems and Apple Carplay. In conclusion, although the design of the Mazda6 Sedan isn't as modern as other designs, it provides a plethora of utilities and can be incredibly aesthetic in certain colors.
