About

(All information is as of December 31, 2022)

Mercedes-Benz Group AG, better know as Mercedes-Benz or simply Mercedes, is a multinational automotive corporation based in German and headquartered Stuggart, Baden-Württemberg. Mercedes-Benz is one of the world's leading car manufacturers and global suppliers of high-end passenger cars and premium vans. It was founded in 1886 by Gottlieb Daimler and Carl Benz and had a total revenue of €150,017 million in 2022. Mercedes-Benz Group AG's brands are Mercedes-Benz, Mercedes-AMG, Mercedes-Maybach, Mercedes me, Mercedes-Benz Bank, Mercedes-Benz Financial Services, and Athlon. It has 170,000 employees and it's automotive sales were 2,456,063 in 2022. Mercedes-Benz Group has 281 locations worldwide with more than 112 of it's locations being in Germany.

Sustainability

From Mercedes-Benz Sustainability Report 2022 and Mercedes-Benz Official Websites

Mercedes-Benz views shaping the future of mobility in a safe and sustainable manner as motivation and duty. Therefore, the company focus's on innovative and green technologies alongside safe and high-quality vehicles. Mercedes countinues to invest and develop for an all-electric future. It's sustainability targets and initiatives are as follows:

  • Mercedes-Benz is shifting from electric-first to electric-only and is accelerating towards a fully-electric and software driven future.
  • Mercedes-AMG's Formula One Team's aim is to become the world's most sustainable team with technological innovation, pioneering spirit and moral obligation and recieved a 21% reduction in overall air travel emissions and 46% reduction in race team travel emissions in 2022.
  • Mercedes-Benz is building it's own battery recycling factory to conserve valuable resources which will recieve a recycling rate of over 96%.
  • Mercedes-Benz targets to cover more than 70% of the energy demand in production with renewable energies (especially through the expansion of solar and wind energy at it's sites) by 2030.

Mercedes-AMG GT Coupé

details

The AMG GT Coupé is an 4-seater luxurious sports car with performance seats made in fine Nappa Leather and can be easily driven on a road or race track. It has aluminium steering wheel gearshift paddles and convenient AMG buttons on the steering wheel. It also offers 2 AMG 5-twin-spoke design, light-alloy wheels.

Review:
The sports car is easy and smooth to drive while in comfortable seats. However, the back 2 seats in the car do not offer as much space and can get cramped for taller passengers or depending on the amount of luggage. However, it provides good sceens, menus and dynamic control dials. Although depending on the series, it can cost from $170,000 - $400,000, owning a mercedes can be more reliable and trustworthy to take care of than a Ferrari or Porsche. Therefore, although the GT Coupé is extremely expensive and can get cramped in the back seats, it gives comfort and reliability for a high-end car.

Mercedes-GLE SUV

details

The Mercedes GLE-Class SUV is a powerful and sporty SUV. It presents a bright panoramic sliding sunroof and a high level of comfort in the ambiance and seats. It also offers the MBUX multimedia system with intuitive operations by voice, touch or gestures. The SUV is also extremely high-tech with a head-up display, USB ports and a Burmester Surround Sound System implemented.

Review:
The GLE-Class SUV is extremely aesthetic and visually appealing, with numerous implementations of modern technology and is equipped with an optional air suspension that can provide increased ground clearance. In terms of cost, the GLE-Class is also more affordable, starting from around $60,000 USD. However, it offers a limited warranty and Mercedes offers no complementary maintainence compared to its counterparts BMW or Porsche. Therefore, although the Mercedes GLE-Class SUV has various modern features and is extremely well-designed, it's maintainance for the future should also be considered.

Mercedes-Benz E-Class Sedan

details

The E-Class Sedan is a progressive design with power domes, restyled head lamps and modern wheel designs. It's dynamic front section is exquisite and stylish and the design implements a panoramic sliding sunroof. The interiors provide a digital cockpit and MBUX, a wide screen cockpit, ambient lighting with 64 different colors to create numerous color themes and a multifunctioning steering wheel in nappa leather.

Review:
The exterior of the E-Class Sedan has a strong personality and a visually appealing design. On the road it is subtle, yet strong and has good LED headlights. However, the interiors of the car are it's highlight and it is full of the latest technology and the seats are designed to address the comfort of passengers. In terms of cost, it can range from $120,000 - $150,000, but is extremely spacious. Therefore, although the E-Class Sedan might be slightly more expensive, it addresses comfort, aesthetics, technology and safety all in one design.
